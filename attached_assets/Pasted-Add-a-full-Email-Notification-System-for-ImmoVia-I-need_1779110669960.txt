1. Welcome email for new Client

2. Welcome email for new Service Provider
Trigger: when a Service Provider creates an account

3. Project submitted email
Trigger: when a Client posts a new project

4. New project available email
Trigger: when a project matches a Service Provider’s services and location

5. New offer received email
Trigger: when a Service Provider sends an offer to a Client

6. New message email
Trigger: when one user sends a message to another user

7. Low ImmoCredits email
Trigger: when a Service Provider has less than 5 ImmoCredits

8. ImmoCredits purchase confirmation email
Trigger: when a Service Provider buys ImmoCredits through mocked checkout

9. Subscription activated email
Trigger: when a Service Provider activates a paid plan

10. Profile approved email
Trigger: when admin approves a Service Provider profile

11. Profile rejected email
Trigger: when admin rejects a Service Provider profile

- Create reusable email templates.
- Do not hardcode email text inside business logic.
- Add email logs to the database.
- If sending an email fails, do not break the app flow.
